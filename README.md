## ğŸ¯ What Is Peasant?

Peasant is a modular financial super app designed for working-class people. It teaches you the financial tricks that wealthy people already know and gives you the tools to escape the paycheck-to-paycheck trap.

### The Mission

**Stop being poor because you don't know what rich people know.**

Peasant breaks down the financial gatekeeping and gives you:
- The knowledge wealthy people learn from their parents
- The tools financial advisors charge thousands for
- The tricks that keep you poor (and how to stop them)
- A community of people who get it

---

## ğŸ§© The Plugin Ecosystem

Peasant is built on a modular plugin system. Each plugin solves one specific financial problem.

**Core Plugins:**
1. **Budget Repair & Balance** - Track spending, kill subscriptions, optimize pay periods
2. **Credit Repair Toolkit** - From 580 to 750 credit score
3. **Debt Destroyer** - Get out of debt without bankruptcy
4. **Car Buying Mastery** - Never get ripped off at a dealership
5. **Health Insurance Optimizer** - HSA vs PPO, stop overpaying
6. **Investment Starter** - From peasant to investor
7. **Side Hustle Tracker** - Turn skills into cash
8. **Home Buying Calculator** - Rent vs buy, the real math
9. **Fast Food Killer** - Scan receipts, get recipes, save $6K+/year

**Platform Plugins:**
10. **Peasant Pay** - Payments and transfers
11. **Peasant Banking** - Your bank, but better
12. **Peasant University** - Financial education
13. **Peasant Community** - Connect with other peasants

See [PLUGINS.md](./PLUGINS.md) for complete details.
