## ðŸ”’ Legal & Compliance

### Required for Paid App

**Privacy Policy** (Required)
- What data you collect
- How you use it
- How you protect it
- User rights
- Contact information

**Terms of Service** (Recommended)
- What app does/doesn't do
- User responsibilities
- Liability limitations
- Refund policy

- Apple handles refunds
- You don't control this
- Users can request refund within 90 days

**Financial Disclaimers**
- Not financial advice
- For informational purposes
- User responsible for financial decisions
- No guarantees of savings

### App Store Guidelines

Must comply with:
- No misleading claims
- No promises of specific results
- Accurate screenshots
- Honest description
- Proper age rating
- Privacy compliance
